# Review Checklist

Before delivery, verify:

1. The content brief is present and sufficient.
2. Every visible scientific claim is traceable to user input or source material.
3. The central visual claim is clear within 5 seconds.
4. The slide has one dominant visual flow.
5. All text is editable PowerPoint text.
6. All visuals are native PowerPoint vectors.
7. No `ppt/media` assets, picture objects, OLE objects, external links, audio, or video are present.
8. No unsupported numbers or mechanisms were invented.
9. The palette is one of the approved presets.
10. Chinese and bilingual text obeys the language rules when applicable.
11. The quality audit script has no blocking issues.
