# Review Checklist

## Preview image
- Does it contain no more than four main modules?
- Does it avoid a plain row of boxes?
- Is there one dominant visual core?
- Are there at least three distinct scientific visual object types?
- Is the mechanism or transformation visible?
- Is a result or evidence cue visible when provided?
- Are large and small frames clearly differentiated?
- Is the information density high enough to avoid a generic poster?
- Is the layout academically restrained, aligned, balanced, and legible?
- Is the preview suitable as a journal graphical abstract after scaling?

## PPT reconstruction
- Does the PPT match the approved preview?
- Are all visible texts editable?
- Are visual objects rebuilt as editable PowerPoint vectors when possible?
- Are the same module count, hierarchy, palette, arrows, and key message preserved?
- Does the PPT remain within the four-module cap?
